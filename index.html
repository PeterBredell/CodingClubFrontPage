Why we focus on activities?

At the Coding Club, we’ve been sparking innovation and building skills that last. From hosting epic hackathons that churn out award-winning apps to running workshops that turn beginners into confident coders, we’re all about hands-on action. Our members have tackled real-world projects, collaborated with industry mentors, and even snagged internships through connections made right here. With a stash of free pizza-fueled coding nights and a trophy case of competition wins, we’re proud to be the hub for Eduvos’s tech enthusiasts. Ready to join the crew that’s coding the future? Dive in!
‎

A list of our most popular activities

  • Movie nights related to technology.
  • Coding-themed game nights.
  • Networking events with other tech clubs.
  • Teaching coding basics to high school students.
  • Building a robotic system with coding integration.
  • Team projects to build a simple game.

More on our activities and culture:

The Coding Club isn’t just a group—it’s a launchpad. We’ve turned late-night brainstorming into apps that impress recruiters, hosted coding showdowns that leave judges speechless, and led sessions where novices debug like pros. Our crew has teamed up with tech insiders, racked up wins in national coding contests, and created a vibe where creativity thrives (with a side of snacks). From mastering algorithms to forging lifelong connections, we’re the place where Eduvos students level up.
